# Cryptonite-MIT DFIR Gita

## Overview
A living resource list for DFIR trainees, providing tools, reference material, and CTF challenges grouped by domain.

## Core Domains
1. **Steganography & File Structures:**
   - Tools: steghide, jsteg, zesteg, openstego, stegseek, stegsolve, deepsound, wavsteg, qrazybox, stegsnow, zwsp-steg, exiftool, binwalk, foremost.
   - Key Challenges: TUCTF 2025 (Bunker), CSAW 2024 (ZIPZIPZIP, The Triple Illusion, Is There An Echo, I Like My Camera RAW), N0PS CTF 2024 (ZipZip), IRONCTF 2024 (Uncrackable Zip), WaniCTF 2024 (tiny10px).

2. **Network & PCAP/Log Analysis:**
   - Tools: Wireshark, tshark, tcpdump, scapy, pyshark.
   - Key Concepts: PCAP vs PCAPNG, field extraction, live capture.
   - Key Challenges: PicoCTF (Wireshark series, WebNet1, Torrent Analyze), WaniCTF 2024 (I wanna be a streamer), NOPSCTF 2024 (HID), PearlCTF 2024 (pcap busterz 1), BCCTF 2025 (Lost City, Times to Die), HITCON CTF 2023 (Not Just usbpcap).

3. **Digital Forensics & Incident Response (DFIR):**
   - Tools: Volatility 2 & 3, Autopsy/Sleuth Kit, FTK Imager, LiME, Sysinternals.
   - Memory Labs: MemLabs (by stuxnet999) is recommended for beginners.
   - Key Challenges: bi0sCTF 2025 (AnansiTap, Bombardino Exfilrino), bi0sCTF 2024 (Batman Investigation II), BITSCTF 2024.

## Additional Resources
- **Blogging/Guides:** Malware & DFIR series by bi0s.
- **Books:** "The Art of Memory Forensics".
- **Tool Repos:** regdmp (registry analysis), vol3-plugins.
